Detailed Description

S3FNet is a high-performance network simulator based on S3F Scalable Simulation Framework.

S3FNet is developed with reference to the PRIME SSFNet developed and maintained by Professor Jason Liu's research group at Florida International University, and the iSSFNet simulator developed and maintained by Professor David M. Nicol's research group at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
